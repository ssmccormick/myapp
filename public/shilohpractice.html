Located in Gowanus, Brooklyn, Vance serves Authentic tapas from Catalonia in a modern industrial space.

We also serve beer, wine and spirits.
Make a Reservation

Tapas originated in the Catalonian region of Spain and are small plates typically consumed with wine or vermouth.

We combine traditional preparations with local ingredients for dishes that are authentic to both their Spanish origins and New York setting. Learn more about our story.

Vermouth is the typical accompaniment to tapas and we invite you to sample our extensive and surprising list.

In addition, we offer craft beer, Spanish and American wines, a full bar and a variety of delightful non-alcoholic options.
